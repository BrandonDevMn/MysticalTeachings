Mystical Teachings

Version 1.0.0

A Progressive Web App for searching Magic: The Gathering cards using the comprehensive Scryfall database.

Features

  • Search thousands of Magic cards by name
  • View detailed card information and images
  • Save favorite cards for quick access
  • Browse search history
  • Works offline with cached data

Data & Attribution

Card data provided by Scryfall, a comprehensive Magic: The Gathering card database.

Magic: The Gathering and all related content are property of Wizards of the Coast.

How to Use

  1. Type a card name in the search box
  2. Tap on any card to view detailed information
  3. Use the heart icon to save cards to favorites
  4. Access your search history in the History tab

Privacy & Contact

This app doesn't collect personal data. All search history and favorites are stored locally on your device.
